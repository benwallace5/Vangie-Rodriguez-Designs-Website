Artist

Vangie Rodriguez

Phoenix-based artist, Vangie Rodriguez, has been honing her skills in oil and acrylic painting for the last several years. Primarily self-taught with a family lineage of artists, her talent has been strongly refined with training at Phoenix Center for the Arts.

Vangie's inspiration is widespread, with a love of Hispanic tradition, nature and most recently, pop culture art, incorporating a bring and bold color palette for custom commissions.

Her passion for being an art student combined with her BS in Education allowed for the union of her talent as an art instructor. Vangie delivers her talent on location for at-home paint parties, charitable and team building events.
