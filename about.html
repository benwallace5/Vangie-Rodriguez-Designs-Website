Artist

Vangie Rodriguez

Phoenix based artist, Vangie Rodriguez, has been honing her skills in oil and acrylic painting for the last several years. Primarily self-taugt with a family lineage of artists, her talent has been strongly refined with training at Phoenix Center for the Arts.

Vangie's inspiration is wide spread, with a love of Hispanic tradition, to nature and most recentyly, pop culture art, incorporatiing a bring and bold colo palette for custom commissions.

Her passion of being an art student combined with her BS in Education, allowed for the union of her talent as an art instructor. Vangie delivers her talent on location for at-home paint parties, charitable and team building events.
